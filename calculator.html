Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this roof replacement calculator?

Our calculator uses current Pennsylvania market rates and industry standards to provide an accurate estimate. However, final costs may vary based on specific property conditions, material availability, and other factors that can only be assessed during an on-site inspection.

What factors affect my roof replacement cost?

Key factors include roof size, complexity, material type, quality, pitch, accessibility, removal needs, and your location within Pennsylvania. Our calculator accounts for these variables to provide you with an accurate estimate.

Will my homeowner's insurance cover a roof replacement?

Insurance typically covers roof replacement if the damage is due to an unexpected event or "act of God" such as a storm, fire, or falling tree. Normal wear and tear or age-related deterioration is generally not covered.
