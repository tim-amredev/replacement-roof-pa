Popular Roofing Materials

Explore some of the most popular roofing materials for Pennsylvania homes

Architectural Shingles

Premium asphalt shingles with added dimension and durability. Perfect for most homes with a lifespan of 25-30 years.

  • Dimensional appearance
  • Enhanced durability
  • Wide variety of colors

Metal Roofing

Long-lasting and energy-efficient option with a modern look. Can last 40-70 years with minimal maintenance.

  • Exceptional longevity
  • Energy efficient
  • Weather resistant

Slate Roofing

Premium natural stone roofing with unmatched beauty and longevity. Can last 100+ years with proper installation.

  • Century-long lifespan
  • Elegant appearance
  • Environmentally friendly

Cedar Shakes

Natural wood roofing with rustic charm and excellent insulation properties. Typically lasts 20-40 years.

  • Natural beauty
  • Good insulation
  • Unique character
