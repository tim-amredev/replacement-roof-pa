Absolutely not. Our instant roof price calculator is completely free to use with no obligation. You can use it to get a general idea of costs before deciding whether to proceed with your project.

How soon can I get my roof replaced after submitting my information?

After submitting your information, a local roofing professional will contact you within 24 hours to schedule an inspection. Depending on their schedule and the season, your roof replacement could begin within 1-3 weeks.

Do you offer financing options for roof replacements?

Yes, many of our partner contractors offer financing options to help make your roof replacement more affordable. Options include low-interest payment plans, zero-down financing, and more. The contractor will discuss these options with you directly.

What types of roofing materials do you recommend?

The best roofing material depends on your home's architecture, your budget, and your preferences. Asphalt shingles are the most popular due to their affordability and versatility. Metal roofing offers excellent durability and energy efficiency. Tile and slate provide premium aesthetics and longevity but at a higher cost.
