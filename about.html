About Replacement Roof PA

Your trusted partner for quality roof replacements across Pennsylvania

Our Story

Founded in 2003, Replacement Roof PA has grown from a small family business to one of Pennsylvania's most trusted roofing companies. Our mission has remained the same: to provide homeowners with exceptional roof replacement services that enhance the beauty, value, and protection of their homes.

With over 20 years of experience and thousands of successful projects completed, we've built our reputation on quality craftsmanship, superior materials, and outstanding customer service.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Fully licensed, bonded, and insured professionals
  • Manufacturer-certified installers for all major roofing brands
  • Comprehensive warranties on materials and workmanship
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • Dedicated project managers for every roof replacement
