Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements take 1-3 days, depending on the size and complexity of your roof, as well as weather conditions.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer several financing options to help make your roof replacement more affordable, including 0% interest plans for qualified homeowners.

Will you work with my insurance company?

Absolutely. Our team has extensive experience working with insurance companies on storm damage claims and can help guide you through the process.

What types of warranties do you offer?

We provide manufacturer warranties on all materials (typically 25-50 years) and our own 10-year workmanship warranty on all installations.
